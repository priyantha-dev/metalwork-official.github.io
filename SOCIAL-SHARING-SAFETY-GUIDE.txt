SOCIAL SHARING SAFETY GUIDE

No website can guarantee protection from reports or platform restrictions. The safest approach is transparent, relevant and non-repetitive sharing.

DO
1. Post only in relevant job groups, pages or professional networks.
2. Use your real identity and clearly state that you are the recruitment contact.
3. Describe the specific job category instead of using exaggerated claims.
4. Use the full website URL; avoid link shorteners and redirect chains.
5. Vary the caption naturally and add useful context for each audience.
6. State that availability and final conditions must be confirmed.
7. State that no job or visa is guaranteed.
8. Reply professionally to genuine questions and correction requests.
9. Remove outdated posts and closed vacancies.
10. Keep screenshots and records of confirmed vacancies and employer-side information.

DO NOT
1. Do not mass-post the same link repeatedly in a short time.
2. Do not use bots, automated comments, automated DMs or purchased engagement.
3. Do not tag unrelated people or groups.
4. Do not claim guaranteed jobs, guaranteed visas, guaranteed appointments or guaranteed entry.
5. Do not request passports, IDs, visas, medical or banking documents in public comments or groups.
6. Do not collect payment through this website.
7. Do not misrepresent the employer, company authorisation or vacancy conditions.
