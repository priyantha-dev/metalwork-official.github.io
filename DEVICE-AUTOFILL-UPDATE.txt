MetalWork Jobs — Device Autofill Update
Version: v18.4

Implemented:
- Browser-native autofill tokens for full name, email, country and city.
- Optional "Use my device location" control for city and country only.
- Explicit browser permission is required before location access.
- Reverse geocoding uses OpenStreetMap Nominatim after the user presses the location button.
- Coordinates are not stored by this website.
- Existing saved-profile, selected-job, message-preview and WhatsApp flows are preserved.
- Same-device profile restore remains limited to the same browser and website domain.

Important privacy limitation:
A website cannot silently read a person's name or email from a device. The browser may suggest details the user has previously saved, and the user must choose or confirm them.
