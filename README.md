## Social-media job links
The website can preselect a vacancy from a Facebook, WhatsApp, TikTok, Telegram or LinkedIn post by adding a `job` query parameter. Example:

`https://priyantha-dev.github.io/metalwork-official.github.io/?job=MIG%2FMAG%20Welder`

A category can also be opened with `?category=Painter`. The application form never sends automatically; the candidate reviews the prepared WhatsApp message and presses Send in WhatsApp.

## Browser autofill
The form includes standard autocomplete fields for full name, email, country and city. Browsers may offer their own autofill. Candidates can also opt in to save their profile locally on the current device.
